Brownie

Description

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup + 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup melted chocolate chips
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips (optional, for extra chocolatey goodness)

Instructions

    Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F) Line a baking pan (approximately 20cm x 30cm) with parchment paper. Melt the butter and chocolate (you can use the microwave, heating slowly in 30-second intervals, stirring until smooth). Add the white sugar and mix well. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, and add the vanilla extract. Mix in the salt and cocoa powder. Finally, add the flour and stir until well combined. Spread the batter evenly in the prepared pan. Bake for about 25-30 minutes. The brownies should be moist but not raw when tested with a toothpick. Let them cool before cutting into squares.
